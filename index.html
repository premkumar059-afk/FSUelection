My Journey

I’m Rohit Yadav, a student at Mahendra Morang Aadarsha Bahumukhi Campus, and like all of you, I dream of a better, more vibrant campus. My academic journey has taken me through Microbiology and BASW, and I’m currently pursuing BBS — adapting to changes, but never losing focus on my goals..

More than just attending classes, I’ve always believed in stepping up for what matters. I worked to solve the BBA faculty’s book shortage and took the lead in organizing the BSc orientation program. I’ve stood with students during every movement, voicing our concerns and making sure our demands were heard through press statements. These experiences have taught me that true leadership isn’t about promises — it’s about action..

If I earn your trust, I’m committed to real changes. I will work to establish a Health Post on campus, ensuring we all have access to basic healthcare. I plan to improve classroom conditions, secure sports areas, and push for bus services to make commuting easier. Strengthening hostel management and ensuring practical materials for science students are also at the top of my list. My goal is simple — to build a campus where every student feels heard, where problems are solved, and where we grow not just academically, but as a community. With your support, we can turn these ideas into reality. Let’s move forward together. ✊

Why I'm Running

I am running for FSU President because I believe in the power of our students and the untapped potential of our campus. I have experienced the struggles we face — from lacking proper facilities to the need for better career support — and I am determined to tackle these challenges with action, not just words.

My candidacy is about bringing a fresh approach and real solutions. I am not here to make empty promises; I am here with a clear plan to create a better learning environment, strengthen student opportunities, and build a campus that empowers us all for a brighter future.

My Vision

My plan for transforming Morang Aadarsha Multiple Campus,Biratnagar focuses on practical improvements that will enhance student life and academic experience.

Transparent Administration

Ensure fair accounting of student funds with regular public audits and transparent decision-making processes.

Internship & Job Opportunities

Establish partnerships with industry leaders to provide internship and job placement opportunities for students.

Improved Lighting

Install adequate lighting throughout campus for safety and to facilitate evening study sessions.

Clean Drinking Water

Ensure access to clean drinking water with filtered water stations across campus.

Better Facilities

Renovate toilets, improve canteen quality, and enhance common areas for student comfort.

Academic Excellence

Advocate for improved teaching methods, updated curriculum, and better laboratory equipment.

Streamlined Administration

Simplify administrative processes for students, reducing bureaucracy and wait times.

Better Internet

Upgrade campus WiFi infrastructure for reliable, high-speed internet access throughout campus.

My Commitment to You

As your FSU President, I pledge to work tirelessly to implement these improvements and be a strong advocate for student rights and welfare. My administration will be characterized by:

Transparency

Regular updates on FSU activities, financial reports, and decision-making processes will be made available to all students.

Accountability

I will take personal responsibility for the success of our initiatives and be answerable to the student body.

Inclusivity

Every student's voice matters. I will ensure that all decisions consider the diverse needs of our student community.
